Camilla Davila

Founder & Chief Strategist

Camilla founded the Comms Collective to create a space where communications professionals could connect, learn, and grow together. She leads strategy and vision for the network.

With a background in strategic communications and reputation management, she believes in the power of genuine peer exchange and collaborative learning. The Collective reflects that vision: a place where diverse roles, talents, and approaches come together without hierarchy.

When she’s not shaping the Collective’s direction, she’s usually in Brussels or travelling—always with a book and an eye for good design.
