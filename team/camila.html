Camila Costa

Co-founder & Strategy

Camila works with NGOs and foundations on narrative and campaign strategy. She has spent over a decade in Brussels helping organisations tell their stories in ways that cut through noise without sacrificing nuance.

She started the collective to create a space where communicators could learn from each other without the usual posturing. She believes the best work happens when we share practice openly and support each other’s growth.

When she’s not running workshops or advising on messaging, she’s usually reading, walking, or cooking for friends.
