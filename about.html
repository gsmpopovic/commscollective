About

Our story, mission, and what we stand for.

Our story

The Comms Collective began with a simple observation: women in communications in Brussels often work in silos. We wanted a space where we could meet, share practice, and learn from each other without the usual posturing.

We started as a handful of people. Today we’re still small by design—a collective, not an institution. We run workshops and meetups, curate resources, and hold space for honest conversation about how we do our work.

Mission

We exist to support women in communications—and anyone who shares our values—with practical tools, peer learning, and a community that values clarity and collaboration over competition.

Philosophy and values

Clarity over jargon. We believe that good communication is clear communication. We favour plain language and honest messaging.

Evidence over spin. We care about getting the story right. That means grounding our work in facts and being transparent about what we know and don’t know.

Collaboration over competition. We’re here to lift each other up. We share resources, feedback, and opportunities. We don’t gatekeep.

Inclusion. We welcome people at every stage of their career and from every sector—NGOs, institutions, agencies, freelancers, and beyond. If you care about communication, you belong here.
