About

Who we are and why we exist.

Our story

The Comms Collective started as a small group of communicators in Brussels who wanted a space to share practice, learn from each other, and push for clearer and more honest communication in advocacy and policy.

We are not a formal organisation. We are a loose collective: people who care about messaging, narrative, and audience, and who believe that good communication can support better outcomes.

What we believe

We believe in clarity over jargon, evidence over spin, and collaboration over competition. We welcome anyone working in or around communications — from NGOs and institutions to freelancers and independents — who shares that mindset.

Where we are

We are based in Brussels and our events take place here. Our resources and ideas are for anyone, anywhere.
