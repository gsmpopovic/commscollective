Who We Are

The Comms Collective in a nutshell.

The Comms Collective is the space for communications professionals of every stripe. Our field brings together a diverse range of roles, talents, and approaches.

From strategic communications, reputation management, and public relations to advocacy, content creation, and graphic design, our members are just as varied.

All backgrounds, experiences, specializations, and curiosities have a place here.

In the Collective, we seek to create space for genuine peer exchange, mentorship, and learning. Here, members can connect, share insights, and discover opportunities to strengthen their skills and expand their knowledge in a supportive, collaborative environment.
