Membership

Connect. Collaborate. Create.

The Collective is intentionally curated, not a catch-all, spammy listserv. We welcome communications professionals working in or around Brussels who are genuinely committed to growing and contributing to the community.

Join our membership programme — it’s completely free. Don’t miss out on the exclusive “Member’s Only” events in Brussels, happening every quarter.

Member perks

  • Access to flagship events

    Priority access and invitations to our two annual flagship events — expert panels and thematic discussions hosted with leading Brussels partner organisations.

  • Networking meet-ups

    Invitations to all informal networking sessions — quarterly drinks, monthly Coffee Corners, and spontaneous get-togethers throughout the year.

  • The resource hub

    Members-only access to trainings, webinar recordings, templates, toolkits, and the Vendor Hub — curated by the community for the community.

  • The Feed newsletter

    Our quarterly newsletter with updates on events, resources, and what’s happening across the Collective. Short, curated, and worth reading.

  • Community channels

    Access to our members-only WhatsApp community — a space to ask questions, share opportunities, and stay connected between events.

  • Job board Coming soon

    A dedicated job board for communications roles in Brussels — posted by member organisations and vetted for quality.

Ready to join?

We are committed to making the Collective inclusive for experts, fans, and newcomers alike. Get in touch and help us build this community.
