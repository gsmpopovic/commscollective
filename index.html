The Comms Collective

A space for women in communications in Brussels—to learn, share, and connect.

We are a small collective based in Brussels. We believe in clear, honest communication and in supporting each other. Whether you work in advocacy, policy, public affairs, or the arts, you’re welcome here.

We run events, curate resources, and hold space for conversation. No membership fees, no hierarchy—just a community of people who care about how we tell stories and shape narratives.
