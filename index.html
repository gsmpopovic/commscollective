The Comms Collective

A small communications collective based in Brussels.

We bring together people who care about clear, honest, and effective communication. Whether you work in advocacy, policy, or the arts, we offer a space to learn, share, and collaborate.

Join our events, explore our resources, or get in touch to find out how you can be part of the collective.

What we do

We run workshops, talks, and informal meetups on topics like strategic communications, narrative change, and audience engagement. We also curate and publish practical resources for communicators in Brussels and beyond.
